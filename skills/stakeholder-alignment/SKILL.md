# Stakeholder Alignment

Linear's real PRD teardown (which pre-empts objections by reframing the request as a problem before proposing a solution), paired with Planning Cadence's W Framework for role clarity — who has a say, who decides, who's just informed.

## When to use this
- Getting sign-off from stakeholders with competing priorities
- Writing a spec that pre-empts stakeholder objections
- Clarifying who genuinely needs a say vs. just an FYI, using the W Framework's role split
