# North Star Metrics

Measurement theory — validity, reliability, measurement error, and levels of measurement — applied to choosing a North Star metric durable enough to actually ladder a team's work into.

## When to use this
- Choosing a North Star metric for a team or product
- Deciding if a candidate metric is reliable enough to be a North Star
- Connecting a North Star to the sub-metrics that actually ladder into it
