# Leading Org Change

Uma Namasivayam's (Dropbox, Senior Director of Engineering Productivity) 3-year account of leading exactly this kind of change — moving an org from PR-throughput obsession to real product velocity, and what breaks once the original bottleneck (AI adoption) is gone.

## When to use this
- Rolling out a new process or tool org-wide
- Getting buy-in for a change that disrupts existing habits
- Figuring out what new bottleneck emerges once the old one is solved
