# High-Stakes Decisions

The ten-question investment discipline for deciding whether to build something at all, competitive-strategy analysis frameworks, and the consulting discipline of deciding what must be true for a decision to change — then testing that efficiently via root-cause diagnosis and MECE structure.

## When to use this
- Making a hard call with incomplete information
- Structuring how to weigh an irreversible or highly consequential decision
- Root-causing a problem with MECE structure before deciding what to do about it
