# High-Stakes Decisions

Trade-off evaluation, competitive-strategy, and consulting-style problem-framing, applied to a single high-stakes decision.

## When to use this
- Making a hard call with incomplete information
- Structuring how to weigh a irreversible/consequential decision
- Explaining a high-stakes call to stakeholders after the fact
