# Shipping Velocity

A 3-year developer-experience case study on moving from PR throughput to real product velocity, plus a startup-specific take on why visibility (not speed) is usually the real bottleneck.

## When to use this
- Diagnosing why a team ships slower than it should
- Deciding what to invest in to unlock velocity (tooling vs. process)
- Measuring velocity in a way that isn't just PR count
