# Team Culture

Vanessa Lee's (VP Product, Shopify) view that a PM's real job is injecting courage into the team, paired with an Italian parable that explains Total Quality Management through monsters (your customers), a gym (your company), and athletes who train their brains and hearts, not just their muscles.

## When to use this
- Diagnosing a team culture problem
- Deciding what behaviors to explicitly reward or discourage
- Using a parable/story to make a culture point land instead of a slide deck
