# Managing Up

The PM Operating Guide's decision-hierarchy framing for product documents, paired with Vanessa Lee's (VP Product, Shopify, reports directly to CEO Toby Lütke) view that a PM's real job is injecting courage into the room — including when there's no spec to hide behind.

## When to use this
- Preparing an update for a skeptical or senior stakeholder
- Structuring a document as a decision hierarchy so leadership can actually parse it
- Deciding when to push back on leadership vs. defer
