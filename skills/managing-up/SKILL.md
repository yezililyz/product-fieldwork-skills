# Managing Up

The PM Operating Guide's workflows and Courage as a Service's take on real PM job, applied to managing upward specifically.

## When to use this
- Preparing an update for a skeptical or senior stakeholder
- Deciding when to push back vs. defer to leadership
- Building trust with a manager who doesn't know the details
