# Roadmap Prioritization

Both the philosophy (whether to build at all) and the mechanics (interactive tools for sequencing, turning strategy into a portfolio of daily work) of roadmap prioritization.

## When to use this
- Building a prioritized roadmap from a raw backlog
- Explaining a prioritization call to stakeholders
- Sequencing strategy into an actual portfolio of work
