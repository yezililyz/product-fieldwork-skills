# Pricing Strategy

How to price an AI product and what determines its unit economics — pulled from a pricing strategist who's worked with 500+ companies, an infrastructure economist benchmarking GPUs daily, and a major streaming platform's head of product — plus a rigorous seat-vs-usage pricing decision framework with an interactive simulator.

## When to use this
- Deciding between seat-based and usage-based pricing
- Pricing an AI feature when unit economics are still unclear
- Testing a pricing change safely before rolling it out fully
