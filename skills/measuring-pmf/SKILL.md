# Measuring PMF

Measurement theory (validity, reliability, levels of measurement) applied to the specific question of whether PMF exists, paired with data from studying over a thousand onboarding flows as a real retention-based PMF signal.

## When to use this
- Deciding what signal would actually prove PMF, not just early traction
- Distinguishing a promising cohort from real product-market fit
- Using onboarding/retention data as a PMF proxy
