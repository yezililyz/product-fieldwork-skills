# Marketplace Fundamentals

A teardown of an agentic-commerce marketplace layer — the closest real example this library has of marketplace mechanics.

## When to use this
- Understanding how a marketplace connects supply and demand
- Deciding what role a product should play in a marketplace
- Reasoning about marketplace-specific product decisions
