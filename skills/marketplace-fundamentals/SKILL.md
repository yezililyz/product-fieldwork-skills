# Marketplace Fundamentals

A teardown of Lemrock, the connective layer that lets brands be recommended and transacted with inside conversational AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, specialized agents) — its positioning, end-to-end architecture, data contracts, and a founding-PM operating plan.

## When to use this
- Understanding how a marketplace connects supply and demand
- Deciding what role a product should play inside a conversational-AI commerce layer
- Reasoning through a founding-PM operating plan for a new marketplace
