# Marketing Org & Stack

A complete beginner-to-confident walkthrough of HubSpot CRM — navigation, contacts, companies, deals, tickets, properties, importing, and one-on-one/marketing emails, all on the free plan.

## When to use this
- Setting up or onboarding a team onto HubSpot CRM specifically
- Structuring how marketing/CRM data connects to product and sales
- Choosing what to actually track in a CRM vs. what's noise
