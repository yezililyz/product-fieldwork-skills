# Retention & Engagement

Data from studying over a thousand onboarding flows — which challenges the conventional wisdom that onboarding should always be short — plus fifteen named onboarding design patterns with real app examples, and the EUREKA product-led-onboarding framework.

## When to use this
- Diagnosing why users churn after an initial burst of activity
- Redesigning onboarding to drive longer-term engagement, even if that means making it longer
- Picking a specific named onboarding pattern that fits the product type
