# Running Meetings

Turns 47 frameworks, templates, and rituals into one meeting operating system: what to kill outright, how to prime attendees beforehand, how to beat groupthink in the room, and how to leave every meeting with a decision and a named owner.

## When to use this
- Designing a weekly team meeting that isn't a waste of time
- Fixing a recurring meeting that's become theater with no real decisions
- Deciding which meetings to cut entirely vs. fix
