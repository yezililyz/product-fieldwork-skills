# Running Meetings

Hundreds of billions of dollars a year die in bad meetings — this is how to clean up how a team meets.

## When to use this
- Designing a weekly team meeting that isn't a waste of time
- Fixing a recurring meeting that's become theater
- Deciding which meetings to cut entirely
