# Positioning

The strategy-as-filter framework (a top-down pyramid, a one-page strategy canvas) paired with competitive-advantage analysis — the consultant's toolkit for external/internal analysis and positioning.

## When to use this
- Writing or sharpening a positioning statement
- Deciding what to lead with in a launch narrative
- Repositioning a product after competitors or the market shifted
