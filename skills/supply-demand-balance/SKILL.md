# Supply/Demand Balance

The Lemrock teardown's data contracts and models for a live agentic-commerce marketplace — the closest real example this library has of supply/demand balancing mechanics in production.

## When to use this
- Diagnosing a supply or demand shortage on one side of a platform
- Deciding which side of a marketplace to invest in first
- Understanding real supply/demand mechanics from a production marketplace example
