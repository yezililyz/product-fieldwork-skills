# Supply/Demand Balance

The agentic-commerce teardown is the closest real example this library has of supply/demand mechanics in a live marketplace.

## When to use this
- Diagnosing a supply or demand shortage on a platform
- Deciding which side of the marketplace to invest in first
- Understanding a real example of marketplace supply/demand design
