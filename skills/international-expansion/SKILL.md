# International Expansion

Distills how Airbnb, Booking.com, Duolingo, TikTok, Uber, Revolut, and Facebook Marketplace timed, targeted, and executed their international launches — since geographic expansion is one of the biggest growth accelerants a company gets, and you usually only get to fire it once.

## When to use this
- Choosing which country/region to expand into next
- Sequencing a multi-market expansion plan
- Learning from how a specific real company (Airbnb, Duolingo, Uber, etc.) approached its own international launch
