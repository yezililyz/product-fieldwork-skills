# Founder Psychology

Saras Sarasvathy's effectuation logic — expert entrepreneurs start from who they are, what they know, and whom they know, then ask what they can do, rather than starting from a fixed goal — paired with the mindset half of the Founder's Sales Playbook (Og Mandino's discipline for showing up every day).

## When to use this
- Working through founder doubt around a hard, ambiguous decision
- Reframing a problem the way expert entrepreneurs do — starting from means, not a fixed goal
- Building the discipline a long, uncertain build/sales cycle requires
