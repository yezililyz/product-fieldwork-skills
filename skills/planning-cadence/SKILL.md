# Planning Cadence

Synthesized from 13 sources across Lenny's Podcast and Newsletter — how Figma, Shopify, Snowflake, Gong, Notion, Linear, Ramp, and Mixpanel actually structure annual, quarterly, and weekly planning, plus the W Framework in full (who owns context vs. plans vs. integration vs. buy-in) and jwegan's portfolio model for growth teams.

## When to use this
- Designing a planning cadence for a team or company
- Fixing a planning process that's too heavy or too loose
- Clarifying who decides what, and when, using the W Framework
- Comparing how a specific real company (Figma, Shopify, Linear, etc.) structures its planning
