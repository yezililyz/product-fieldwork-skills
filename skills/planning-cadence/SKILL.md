# Planning Cadence

A field guide to planning cadence — how top companies structure annual, quarterly, and weekly planning, plus the W Framework and portfolio thinking for growth teams.

## When to use this
- Designing a planning cadence for a team or company
- Fixing a planning process that's too heavy or too loose
- Clarifying who decides what, and when, in planning
