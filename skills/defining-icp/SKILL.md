# Defining ICP

The go-to-market field guide's core insight that who you sell to is one of the handful of decisions that decides whether you hit your first few million in two years or seven — the closest grounding this library has for ICP work specifically.

## When to use this
- Defining an ICP for a new product or segment
- Deciding which segment to prioritize for GTM
- Refining ICP based on which customers actually convert and retain
