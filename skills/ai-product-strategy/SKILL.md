# AI Product Strategy

Draws on 34 conversations with the people building the AI era at Anthropic, OpenAI, Microsoft, Cursor, Windsurf, Scale, Surge, Intercom, LinkedIn, and Slack, plus AI/ML PM wisdom from leaders at Amazon, Meta, Google, PayPal, and Booking.com. Covers why 95% of AI projects fail — teams start with tools instead of problems — and the strategic, financial, technical, and career frameworks that put you in the other 5%, including when ML is even the right tool for a problem in the first place.

## When to use this
- Deciding whether/how to add AI to an existing product
- Setting strategy for a net-new AI product
- Working out if a problem actually calls for ML at all, vs. rules or heuristics
- Understanding what's different about PMing an ML feature vs. a normal one
