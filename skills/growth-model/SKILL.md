# Growth Model

Real ARPU growth and retention diagnostics from Toggl's 2.0 product work, data from studying over a thousand onboarding flows (which challenges the conventional wisdom that shorter onboarding is always better), and the EUREKA product-led-onboarding framework fused with real AI-industry GTM data points.

## When to use this
- Mapping out a product's growth loop end to end
- Diagnosing which stage of the funnel is actually broken
- Deciding if onboarding should actually be longer, against conventional wisdom
