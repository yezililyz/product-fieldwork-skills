# Analyzing User Feedback

Covers turning rich, messy user feedback into measurable, trackable insight leaders can act on — without oversimplifying it — plus the underlying measurement theory: validity, reliability, measurement error, and the different levels of measurement used in quantitative research.

## When to use this
- Synthesizing themes out of a pile of support tickets or interview notes
- Deciding what counts as valid vs. noisy signal in qualitative data
- Quantifying something that started as a qualitative observation
- Checking whether a proposed metric is actually measuring what you think it is
