# Acquisition Channels

Covers identifying, testing, and scaling the channels that drive sustainable growth — from a product's first 1,000 users to a world-class primary engine, with real measurement discipline for knowing what's actually working. Also covers Clay specifically: sourcing, enriching, AI personalization, waterfalls, formulas, and the campaign architectures that let a small team do what used to take an army of SDRs.

## When to use this
- Picking which channel to test first for a new product
- Diagnosing why a channel that used to work has stopped working
- Setting up Clay-style enrichment/personalization for outbound
- Building a repeatable channel-testing measurement process
