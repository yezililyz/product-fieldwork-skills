# Founder Sales

Two timeless books decoded for founders: the mindset to keep showing up, and the mechanics of the sale itself.

## When to use this
- A founder making their first sales calls
- Building conviction/resilience for founder-led sales
- Structuring a founder-led sales process before hiring sales
