# Founder Sales

Two timeless books decoded for founders: Og Mandino's mindset for showing up every day, and Rob Fitzpatrick's Mom Test method for learning the truth from customer conversations instead of flattering lies.

## When to use this
- A founder making their first sales calls
- Building the resilience/mindset for founder-led sales
- Running customer conversations that surface the truth, not politeness
